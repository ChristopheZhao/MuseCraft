## 3. Hard Constraints
- `scene_info_ref` remains authoritative media-input membership only.
- WM structured execution facts / receipts remain the execution-fact source for progress.
- `scene_outputs.*` remains artifact/reference output only.
- The progress file/read-model must be derived, rebuildable, and non-authoritative.
- No planner-owned completion-state writer may be introduced.
- No duplicate-gate compatibility layer may remain in the accepted mainline.
- `scene_info_ref` is treated in this slice as a persisted boundary input reference for downstream media planning and execution.
- Its payload may be assembled from upstream stage outputs, but once assembled for the current media-generation slice it is treated as a frozen boundary input snapshot.
- Planned scene membership for this slice is read from `scene_info_ref.scenes_to_generate[]`.
- Current-slice execution facts and `scene_outputs.*` may inform a derived progress read-model, but they may not rewrite planned membership authority.
- If the progress file/read-model cannot be rebuilt from its source facts, the degradation may remain non-authoritative but it must be explicitly diagnosable in planner input rather than silently disappearing.
